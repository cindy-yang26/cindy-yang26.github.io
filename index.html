Fun Facts

Vermont has no roadside billboards by law, so highways feel calm and scenic.
Montpelier is the smallest U.S. state capital by population.
The Long Trail opened in 1910 and later inspired part of the Appalachian Trail idea.
Lake Champlain has more than 70 islands and a famously colorful sunrise.
Maple syrup season is called "sugaring," and timing depends heavily on freeze-thaw cycles.
Autumn foliage can peak quickly, which makes timing leaf season almost a local sport.
